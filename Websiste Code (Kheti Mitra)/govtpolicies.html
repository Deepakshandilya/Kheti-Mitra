Government Policies on Agriculture

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN)

This is a central government scheme that provides direct income support to small and marginal farmers. Under this scheme, eligible farmers receive financial assistance of Rs. 6,000 per year in three equal installments.

National Agriculture Market (e-NAM)

The e-NAM is an electronic trading platform that aims to create a unified national market for agricultural commodities. It allows farmers to sell their produce online and provides better price discovery and transparency.

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

PMFBY is a crop insurance scheme that provides financial protection to farmers in case of crop loss due to natural calamities. Both farmers and the government contribute to the premium, making it affordable for farmers.
